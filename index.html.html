Suffixed words are found automatically when you parse chapters. Includes verbs with object suffixes (וְכִבְשֻׁהָ), nouns with possessive suffixes (בֵּיתוֹ), and prepositions with suffixes (אֵלָיו). Note: construct-state forms like פְּנֵי ("face of") are grammatically distinct from possessive suffixes — they take a following genitive noun rather than standing alone. The AI prompt now explicitly excludes these, but occasional false positives may still appear.
